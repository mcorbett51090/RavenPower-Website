# Raven Power Website Proposal

**Recommended Platform:** Webflow (Path A)

## Why Webflow for Raven Power

- You are not a full-time web developer and want to maintain the site easily.
- Most of your ideal clients (professional services, consulting, mid-market) benefit from a platform where they or a VA can update content without code.
- Webflow provides professional design quality and strong performance while keeping long-term maintenance reasonable.

## Core Positioning

Raven Power helps teams build reliable, well-architected Power Platform and custom agentic AI systems — with a strong focus on long-term maintainability, governance, and doing things correctly the first time.
