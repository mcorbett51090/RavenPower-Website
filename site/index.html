About

Founded by Matt Corbett.

MC

Raven Power LLC is a Bermuda boutique pairing financial-regulatory discipline with modern agentic tooling. Founder Matt Corbett spends his days as a senior data analyst in the financial-regulatory sector — work that demands precision, auditability, and a clear paper trail.

Raven Power brings that same discipline to engagements built on Anthropic's Claude, Microsoft Power Platform, Power BI, and Microsoft Fabric. The goal is plain: solve the technological problem in front of you quickly, and leave the client with something they can run themselves.

Matt holds an MBA from the University of South Alabama and is Microsoft-certified across the Power Platform and Fabric stack. Connect on LinkedIn.
